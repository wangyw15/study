# 5. 以下为十六进制数，用二进制补码计算并用十六进制表示结果

## (1) $(-85)+76$

$$
\begin{aligned}
-85_{(16)}=&101111011_{(2)} \\
76_{(16)}=&001110110_{(2)}
\end{aligned} \\
101111011+001110110=111110001 \\
111110001_{(2)}=-F_{(16)}
$$

## (2) $85+(-76)$

$$
\begin{aligned}
85_{(16)}=&010000101_{(2)} \\
-76_{(16)}=&110001010_{(2)}
\end{aligned} \\
010000101+110001010=00001111 \\
00001111_{(2)}=F_{(16)}
$$

## (3) $85-76$

$$
85-76=85+(-76) \\
\begin{aligned}
85_{(16)}=&010000101_{(2)} \\
-76_{(16)}=&110001010_{(2)}
\end{aligned} \\
010000101+110001010=000001111 \\
00001111_{(2)}=F_{(16)}
$$

## (4) $85-(-76)$

$$
85-(-76)=85+76 \\
\begin{aligned}
85_{(16)}=&010000101_{(2)} \\
76_{(16)}=&001110110_{(2)}
\end{aligned} \\
010000101+001110110=011111011 \\
011111011{(2)}=FB_{(16)}
$$

## (5) $(-85)-76$

$$
(-85)-76=(-85)+(-76) \\
\begin{aligned}
-85_{(16)}=&101111011_{(2)} \\
-76_{(16)}=&110001010_{(2)}
\end{aligned} \\
101111011+110001010=100000101 \\
100000101_{(2)}=-FB_{(16)}
$$

## (6) $(-85)-(-76)$

$$
(-85)-(-76)=(-85)+76 \\
\begin{aligned}
-85_{(16)}=&101111011_{(2)} \\
76_{(16)}=&001110110_{(2)}
\end{aligned} \\
101111011+001110110=111110001 \\
111110001_{(2)}=-F_{(16)}
$$
